Data collection

The following tools/services are used when visitng our website or using our rss feeds:
  • The website and the rss feed is hosted as a github page on github. We do not have access to this data and refer to Github's Privacy Statement.
  • The website uses Google Analytics. Here we activatd the IP Anonymization feature and the collected data will be deleted after X month.
  • The rss feeds are tracked with Blubrry and we collect anymous data (downloads per episode, OS, Device Type, Location by country). Fore more details we refer to Blubrry's data privacy.

Data usage

  • We only use the collected data by ourselfs to track and improve the quality of our podcast. Escpecially, we are interested in the amount of listeners for each episode, so we can adapt our content to our listeners needs.
  • We do not share or sell the collected data to third parties.
